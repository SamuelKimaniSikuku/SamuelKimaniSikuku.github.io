Shame

2025

Shame is the new currency.

"I think people who were bullied or mocked early in life develop a superpower of not giving a damn about being mocked anymore.

When kids are trained through shame, it can be an effective tool to make them better.

But the problem is that it often remains a bug in them for a long time.

They grow up constantly worrying about how other people view them and always fearing that their social status cannot be risked.

You cannot develop substance, net worth, wealth, or even health unless you are willing to risk your reputation.

Let shame not hold you back."
